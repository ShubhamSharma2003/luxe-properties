About the Corridor

The Newest Luxury Frontier in NCR.

Dwarka Expressway — officially the Northern Peripheral Road, designated NH-248BB — is a 29 kilometre, 8-lane, elevated expressway connecting IGI Airport's Dwarka end in Delhi to Gurgaon's western sectors and onward to NH-48. The Gurgaon section was inaugurated in March 2024 by Prime Minister Modi; the Delhi section completed shortly after. The full corridor is now operational end-to-end.

What this infrastructure unlocked is unprecedented in Indian luxury real estate: sixteen residential sectors that, between them, host the largest concurrent marquee-developer launch pipeline NCR has ever seen. M3M, Sobha, Birla, Godrej, Hero Homes, Smart World, Conscient, Adani, Signature Global — every brand that matters has staked a position here. The launches range from premium 3 BHK at ₹2.5 Crore to ultra-luxury 4 BHK at ₹12 Crore-plus.

The corridor's economic logic is simple. Dwarka Expressway delivers brand-new infrastructure at pricing 40–60% below the established Golf Course Extension Road belt. For investors with a 5-7 year horizon, and end-users who prioritise airport connectivity over Cyber City proximity, it represents the strongest medium-term appreciation play in NCR.

Investment Thesis

Why DXP Is the Strongest Medium-Term Play.

  • Brand-new infrastructure, already deliveredThe 2024 inauguration removed the single biggest risk factor in Indian luxury real estate — whether the promised infrastructure would actually arrive. It has. Future appreciation is now pure demand-driven, not infrastructure-driven.
  • Largest marquee developer concentration in NCRM3M, Sobha, Birla, Godrej, Hero, Smart World, Conscient, Adani, Signature Global — every brand of consequence is launching here. Brand quality drives both end-user demand and rental yield premium.
  • 40–60% pricing discount to established corridorsFor comparable build quality and amenities, DXP delivers larger floor plates per crore than Golf Course Extension Road or Golf Course Road. Entry economics are structurally favourable for investors.
  • Direct IGI Airport connectivityFor frequent travellers, NRI buyers, aviation professionals and globally-mobile executives, DXP delivers a single-corridor advantage no other Gurgaon location can replicate. This sustains a structurally unique buyer pool.
  • Proposed Metro extensionThe Metro extension along Dwarka Expressway is in active planning stages. When delivered, it will replicate Golf Course Road's Rapid Metro advantage — another step-function appreciation trigger ahead.
  • Social infrastructure maturing in 5–7 yearsSchools, hospitals, retail and dining infrastructure is currently sparse but is building rapidly. The 2030 corridor will look meaningfully different from the 2026 corridor — this is the structural appreciation thesis.
Frequently Asked Questions

Dwarka Expressway — Answered.

Dwarka Expressway (also known as Northern Peripheral Road or NH-248BB) is a 29 km elevated 8-lane expressway connecting IGI Airport's Dwarka end in Delhi to Gurgaon's western sectors and NH-48. Inaugurated in March 2024, it has unlocked 16+ residential sectors (99-115) and is now the largest concurrent luxury residential launch pipeline in NCR.
The luxury residential sectors on Dwarka Expressway are 99, 102, 103, 104, 106, 107, 108, 109, 110, 111, 112, 113 and 114. Sectors 102–104 and 113–114 host the highest concentration of marquee launches. Sector 113 in particular is emerging as the corridor's premium pocket with M3M Mansion and Smart World One DXP.
As of 2026, residential prices on Dwarka Expressway range from ₹9,000 to ₹18,000 per square foot, with premium pockets like Sector 113 commanding ₹15,000–18,000 psf. Total ticket sizes range from ₹2.5 Crore for 3 BHK to ₹12+ Crore for 4 BHK ultra-luxury launches like M3M Mansion.
Dwarka Expressway provides direct 8-lane elevated connectivity to IGI Airport (15–20 minutes), Aerocity, and Delhi via Dwarka. It connects to NH-48 at the southern end (giving access to Cyber City and central Gurgaon in 15–25 minutes), and to the Urban Extension Road and Northern Peripheral Road network. The proposed Metro extension along the corridor is in planning stages.
Dwarka Expressway is widely considered Gurgaon's strongest medium-term appreciation play. Drivers include: brand-new infrastructure (inaugurated 2024), the largest marquee developer concentration in NCR (M3M, Sobha, Birla, Godrej, Hero, Smart World), 40–60% lower entry pricing than established corridors, and direct airport connectivity. Risks include corridor maturity timeline (5–7 years for full social infrastructure).
Marquee projects include M3M Mansion (Sector 113), Sobha Altus (Sector 106), Godrej Zenith (Sector 89), Birla Navya (Sector 63A adjacent), Hero Homes (Sector 104), Smart World One DXP (Sector 113), Conscient Parq (Sector 80), and Adani Samsara (Sector 60). Pricing ranges from ₹2.5 Cr for 3 BHK at the lower end to ₹12+ Cr for ultra-luxury 4 BHK.
Golf Course Extension Road is the more established luxury corridor with higher pricing, mature social infrastructure, and proximity to Cyber City — better for end-users prioritising lifestyle and shorter horizon resale. Dwarka Expressway offers 40–60% lower entry pricing, larger floor plates per crore, brand-new infrastructure, and direct IGI Airport access — better for investors with a 5–7 year horizon and buyers prioritising airport connectivity over Cyber City proximity.
The Gurgaon section of Dwarka Expressway (19 km) was inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in March 2024. The Delhi section (10 km) was completed and operational by late 2024. The full 29 km corridor is now operational end-to-end, providing direct elevated 8-lane access between IGI Airport and Gurgaon.
