About the Corridor

The Spine of New Gurgaon Luxury.

Golf Course Extension Road — known locally as GCE Road — runs south-east from the terminus of Golf Course Road in DLF Phase 5, threading through sectors 58, 59, 65, 66 and 67 before connecting to the Southern Peripheral Road and Sohna Road. It is the natural extension of Gurgaon's most prestigious residential spine, carrying that prestige forward into a corridor that is both newer and, by most measures, faster-appreciating.

Where Golf Course Road is largely complete, Golf Course Extension Road is in its growth phase. New marquee launches arrive every quarter. Land parcels are larger. FSI utilisation is more disciplined. And the developer concentration — Oberoi, DLF, M3M, Trump, Godrej, Sobha, Birla, Adani — reads like a register of India's most respected residential brands.

The corridor's centre of gravity is the Grand Hyatt Gurugram in Sector 58, around which the city's most ambitious ultra-luxury launches are now clustering. Most prominently: Oberoi Three Sixty North, the brand's first-ever NCR project.

Investment Thesis

Why GCE Road Will Outperform Through 2030.

  • Infrastructure compoundingRapid Metro extension, NH-48 widening, Dwarka Expressway completion, and SPR connectivity each contribute layered upside. Three expressway access points create commute redundancy unmatched in Gurgaon.
  • Marquee developer concentrationOberoi, DLF, M3M, Trump, Godrej, Sobha, Birla, Adani — every brand of consequence is present. Brand quality drives both end-user demand and rental yield premium.
  • Frontage scarcityThe premium Sector 58–65 stretch is largely built out. New large-format launches will increasingly come from adjacent sectors (49, 67, 113), reinforcing the existing belt's exclusivity.
  • Cyber City proximity5–10 minute drive to Cyber City and Cyber Hub keeps the corporate executive end-user demand structurally anchored. This is rare in true ultra-luxury corridors globally.
  • Pre-launch entry economicsThe pace of new launches (Oberoi, M3M, Godrej, Birla) means strong pre-launch entry windows recur every 6–9 months — a structural advantage for investors over established corridors.
  • Rental absorptionFurnished 3 & 4 BHK rentals on the corridor command ₹2.5L–₹6L+ per month and absorb in 2–6 weeks, supporting a healthy gross yield (1.8–2.5%) by Indian luxury standards.
Frequently Asked Questions

Golf Course Extension Road — Answered.

Golf Course Extension Road (GCE Road) is a luxury real estate corridor running south-east from the end of Golf Course Road in Gurgaon, through sectors 58, 59, 65, 66 and 67. It is one of NCR's most actively developed premium residential belts, anchored by the Grand Hyatt and home to projects by Oberoi Realty, DLF, M3M, Trump, Godrej and Sobha.
Sectors 58, 59, 65, 66 and 67 are the primary luxury sectors on Golf Course Extension Road. Sector 58 is the highest-prestige stretch, anchored by the Grand Hyatt and Oberoi Three Sixty North. Sectors 65–66 host Trump Towers, M3M Golf Estate and Sobha-grade development. Sector 49 and Sector 113 are increasingly considered part of the broader corridor.
As of 2026, average residential prices on Golf Course Extension Road range from ₹18,000–₹38,000 per square foot, depending on sector and developer. Sector 58 commands the highest premium at ₹28,000–₹38,000 psf. Ultra-luxury launches such as Oberoi Three Sixty North start at ₹23 Crore for 4 BHK residences.
GCE Road connects directly to Golf Course Road, NH-48, Sohna Road and the Southern Peripheral Road. Cyber City is 5–10 minutes away depending on the sector, IGI Airport is 20–25 minutes, and Rapid Metro stations at sectors 55–56 provide mass transit access. The Dwarka Expressway interchange is approximately 15 minutes away.
Golf Course Extension Road has shown the strongest price appreciation among Gurgaon's luxury corridors over the past decade. Drivers include Rapid Metro extension, NH-48 access, proximity to Cyber City, ongoing infrastructure (SPR, Dwarka Expressway interchange) and a steady pipeline of marquee launches. Frontage in Sectors 58–65 is largely built out, creating structural scarcity for new launches.
Marquee projects include Oberoi Three Sixty North (Sector 58, pre-launch, from ₹23 Cr), M3M Golf Estate (Sector 65), Trump Towers Gurgaon (Sector 65), Sobha International City (Sector 109 adjacent), Godrej Aristocrat (Sector 49) and M3M Mansion (Sector 113). Pricing ranges from ₹3.5 Cr for 3 BHK at the lower end to ₹50+ Cr for ultra-luxury 5 BHK estates.
Golf Course Road (Phase 5) is the more established, higher-base-priced corridor with ready-to-move iconic addresses like DLF Camellias and DLF Crest. Golf Course Extension Road is the newer, faster-appreciating belt with more pre-launch and under-construction options. For end-users prioritising immediate possession and proven address, Golf Course Road. For investors and pre-launch buyers, Golf Course Extension Road has shown stronger CAGR.
