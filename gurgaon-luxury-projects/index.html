The Gurgaon Brief

A market that refuses to compromise.

Gurgaon's luxury market is not a single market. It is five overlapping ones — each with its own buyer, its own developer set, and its own price logic. Golf Course Road trades like Manhattan: thin float, deep waitlists, resale records every quarter. Golf Course Extension Road is where the next decade's Camellias are being built today. Dwarka Expressway is the value play with infrastructure tailwinds. Sohna Road serves the integrated-township and senior-living buyer. And the original DLF Phases hold a small, treasured roster of low-density addresses that almost never come to market.

This page is the spine of how we cover all of it. Every project below has its own deep-dive — pricing, configurations, developer pedigree, location thesis, NRI structuring. Use the corridor cards to navigate by geography, the catalog tabs to navigate by price band, or the developer strip to navigate by name.

Why Gurgaon Luxury

Eight reasons the premium quartile compounds faster than the index.

  1. Land scarcity inside the triangle.The Cyber City–Golf Course Road–GCE Road triangle has fewer than 800 acres of remaining luxury-grade land. Every new launch is on a smaller parcel than the last.
  2. Developer concentration.Six listed developers (DLF, Oberoi, M3M, Sobha, Godrej, Max) control the trophy pipeline. Deep balance sheets, RERA discipline, predictable delivery.
  3. HNI demand depth.Gurgaon hosts the largest concentration of resident HNIs and corporate C-suite households outside Mumbai. End-use demand absorbs new supply within 18–24 months.
  4. NRI structural inflows.NRI buyers from the GCC, US and Singapore have driven 28–34% of luxury primary sales in Gurgaon over the last three years. FEMA permits full repatriation on up to two residential properties.
  1. Resale liquidity at the top.Trophy assets on Golf Course Road change hands at all-time-high rates with limited price discovery — buyers exist for every floor that becomes available.
  2. Infrastructure tailwinds.Dwarka Expressway, Rapid Metro extension, Southern Peripheral Road expansion and the IGI Airport T4 plan all materially compress travel times to luxury micro-markets.
  3. Branded residences.Trump, Oberoi, Sobha, Antara — the branded-residence category commands a 25–40% premium over comparable un-branded inventory and is structurally under-supplied.
  4. Currency-hedged asset class.For NRI buyers, Gurgaon luxury offers rupee asset exposure against a long-term INR depreciation thesis, with rental yields plus capital appreciation outpacing most G7 prime markets.
