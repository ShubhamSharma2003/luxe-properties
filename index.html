Three Addresses. Different Stories.

01

Sectors 42–54 · Gurgaon

This is where Gurgaon luxury became a real category. DLF built The Crest, Magnolias, and Camellias here — projects that set the benchmark for everything that followed. Today, Golf Course Road commands ₹18,000–32,000 per sq.ft and draws buyers who want the most established address in the NCR. Capital values have held through multiple market cycles, which is why investor retention is highest in this corridor.

Honest answers — from 20 years of conversations with buyers exactly like you.

What's the right corridor for luxury property in Gurgaon in 2026?

There isn't one right answer — it depends on your objective. If you want the most established address and the highest resale liquidity, Golf Course Road (Sectors 42–54) is the benchmark. If you want the best value with strong appreciation potential, Golf Course Extension Road (Sectors 55–70) is where we're seeing the most interesting pre-launch opportunities right now. Dwarka Expressway makes most sense for buyers with a longer horizon who want significant capital gains.

What price should I expect for a luxury flat in Gurgaon?

A well-located 3 BHK luxury apartment on Golf Course Extension Road starts at around ₹3.5–5 Crore. On Golf Course Road, 4 BHK units in premium projects typically start from ₹8–12 Crore, with ultra-luxury penthouses going to ₹25–30 Crore and above. The Oberoi Sector 58 project — which we consider a genuine landmark launch — starts at ₹23 Crore for a 4 BHK. Prices move significantly depending on floor, view, and possession timeline.

Can NRIs buy luxury property in Gurgaon easily?

Yes — and we do it routinely. NRIs can purchase residential property in India under FEMA regulations, using NRE or NRO accounts or direct foreign remittances. The process is well-established. What matters is having an advisor who understands the documentation trail, knows how to structure the transaction correctly, and can handle everything remotely without requiring you to be physically present. That's precisely what our NRI desk does.

How do I get pre-launch pricing on upcoming luxury projects in Gurgaon?

Pre-launch access is relationship-based — developers allocate units to channel partners they trust before opening to the market. We have direct allocations with DLF, Oberoi Realty, Godrej Properties, M3M, Sobha, Emaar, and Adani Realty. When a project moves into soft launch, our registered buyers get first call.
