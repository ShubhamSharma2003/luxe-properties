    • Project Overview

    A community designed around the people who built us.

    Max Antara at Sector 36A, Gurgaon is the joint expression of two institutions that have spent decades thinking about well-being. Max Estates, the listed real estate arm of the Max Group, develops the community. Antara Senior Living — launched by the Max Group in 2013 and India's only integrated senior care ecosystem — manages the senior residences and the primary healthcare infrastructure that supports them.

    The current live phase, Estate 361, spans 18.23 acres on Dwarka Expressway. Six towers rising up to 46 floors are distributed across 2,50,000+ sq ft of open greens, a serpentine lake, organic forest, and more than 700 trees. Of the six towers, two are dedicated to senior living residences — a model that places seniors within a vibrant multi-generational community rather than isolating them.

    Three configurations are available: 2.5 / 3 BHK (circa 2,300 sq ft), 3 BHK (circa 2,721 sq ft), and 3.5 / 3 BHK+ (circa 3,463 sq ft). All residences are delivered fully finished with senior-adaptive features — anti-skid tiles, anti-glare lighting, edgeless walls, lowered kitchen slabs, pre-fitted wardrobes, and wall-mounted panic alarms in every unit.

    Pricing starts at approximately ₹5.75 Crore and approximately ₹25,000 psf. Expected possession is 2028–29. For qualified families — particularly NRI children planning for parents in India — Unisel Realty acts as advisory partner, coordinating site visits, documentation, FEMA compliance, and ongoing liaison through possession and beyond.

    The Antara Way of Life

    Seven pillars of wellness.

    Antara is India's only integrated senior care ecosystem — more than a managed community, it is a philosophy of ageing that treats later life as a phase of reflection, re-engagement, and renewed purpose. The Antara Way of Life manifests through seven wellness pillars, embedded into the daily rhythm of the community and the operational design of every service.

    01

    Physical Wellness

    Senior-adapted gym, temperature-controlled indoor pool, outdoor gym, yoga terrace, daily physiotherapy and guided activity under the supervision of Antara's care team.

    02

    Social Wellness

    Daily, weekly, and monthly resident engagement calendars — cultural nights, community dinners, festival programming, and peer-led interest groups.

    03

    Emotional Wellness

    Dedicated emotional wellness programming, counsellor access, companionship support, and intergenerational engagement with families residing in the broader estate.

    04

    Intellectual Wellness

    Reading corner, cards room, theatre, creative workshops, expert-led lectures and learning programmes — the life of the mind, unhurried.

    05

    Occupational Wellness

    Opportunities to teach, mentor, consult and stay professionally engaged — because retirement from work does not mean retirement from purpose.

    06

    Spiritual Wellness

    Meditation spaces, yoga pavilion, dedicated quiet zones and programming that honours individual spiritual practice without imposing a single framework.

    07

    Environmental Wellness

    700+ trees, serpentine lake, organic forest, 2,50,000+ sq ft of open greens — the design honours the simple truth that ageing well happens outdoors, in natural light, on soft ground.

    What is Max Antara Sector 36A Gurgaon?
    Max Antara at Sector 36A is a premium senior living community at Estate 361, developed by Max Estates and exclusively managed by Antara. Spanning 18.23 acres on Dwarka Expressway, the project features six towers (up to 46 floors) of which two are dedicated senior living towers. It follows the success of Estate 360, which is nearly sold out. Residences start at approximately ₹5.75 Crore, with possession targeted for 2028–29.
    Where is Max Antara located?
    Max Antara is located at Sector 36A, on Dwarka Expressway in Gurugram, Haryana. The site offers direct connectivity to the Dwarka Expressway and is approximately 1 km from the upcoming Cyber Hub 2, 20 minutes from IGI Airport, and minutes from the upcoming ISBT. The address blends the green, low-density character of the emerging Dwarka Expressway corridor with full access to Gurgaon's primary business and healthcare infrastructure.

    Is Max Antara only for senior citizens?
    No. Max Antara is part of a broader intergenerational community. The community model is specifically designed so seniors live within a multi-generational ecosystem, rather than isolated in a senior-only enclave — which research consistently shows improves quality-of-life outcomes for elderly residents.
