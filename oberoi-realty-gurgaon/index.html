Oberoi Three Sixty North · Sector 58 Gurgaon

14.5 Acres. Seven Towers. One Address.

Oberoi Three Sixty North marks Oberoi Realty's debut in the Delhi NCR market — a masterplanned ultra-luxury estate spread across 14.5 acres on Golf Course Extension Road, Sector 58, Gurgaon. Seven soaring residential towers, rising to 35 floors with structural provision to extend to 50, house just 700 residences in total, designed exclusively in 4 BHK (5,500 sq ft) and 5 BHK (8,500 sq ft) configurations.

Located directly adjacent to the Grand Hyatt Gurugram, the project sits at the geographic and prestige centre of Gurgaon's most dynamic luxury corridor. Cyber City is five minutes away. Golf Course Road is eight. The Indira Gandhi International Airport is twenty.

What sets Oberoi Sector 58 apart is not the address alone — it is the standard. Seventy-five years of Oberoi hospitality DNA, applied for the first time to residential architecture in North India.

Bare Shell Delivery

Your Canvas. Your Commission.

All Oberoi Three Sixty North residences are delivered bare shell — flooring, joinery, kitchen and bathroom fit-outs are not included by the developer. This is intentional. Why Sector 58

Sector 58 is the geographic pivot point of Gurgaon's luxury map — the moment Golf Course Road transitions into Golf Course Extension Road. It captures the prestige of the established Phase 5 corridor and the appreciation potential of the newer GCE belt simultaneously.

The micromarket is anchored by the Grand Hyatt, the Trump Tower complex, M3M Golf Estate, and now Oberoi Three Sixty North. Frontage is fully built out — there is no further large-format land to be released in this stretch.
