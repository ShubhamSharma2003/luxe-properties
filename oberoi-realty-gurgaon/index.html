Oberoi Realty Exclusive Launch is Oberoi Realty's most ambitious foray into North India — a masterplanned ultra-luxury estate set across 14.5 acres on Golf Course Extension Road, Sector 58, Gurugram, directly adjacent to the Grand Hyatt. Starting at ₹23 Crore, these are residences that announce themselves without elaboration. Oberoi Realty, the creator of iconic projects across Mumbai, now brings that uncompromising standard to the capital's most dynamic luxury corridor.

Seven soaring towers — rising to 35 floors with RERA approval to extend to 50 — house just 700 residences across the entire estate. Not a number arrived at by chance, but by intent. Because at Oberoi Realty Exclusive Launch, space is the rarest luxury of all.
